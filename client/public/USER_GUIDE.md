## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. When you first open StudyFocus, you'll see the **Sign Up** page.
2. Enter your:
   - **Full Name** (as you'd like it to appear)
   - **Email Address** (this will be your login)
   - **Password** (must be at least 6 characters)
   - **Confirm Password** (must match your password)
3. Click **"Create Account"**.
4. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to the Home page.

**Important:** If you already have an account, click **"Sign In"** at the bottom of the signup form to switch to the login page.

### Logging In

1. On the **Login** page, enter your:
   - **Email Address**
   - **Password**
2. Click **"Sign In"**.
3. If you don't have an account yet, click **"Sign Up"** at the bottom.

**Note:** Your login session is saved, so you won't need to log in every time you visit StudyFocus.

## Study Session (Solo)

### Purpose

Start and manage your individual study sessions with customizable timers, break management, and a digital blackboard for notes.

### Features

- **Multiple Study Techniques**: Pomodoro, Short (15 min), Medium (45 min), Long (90 min), or Custom duration
- **Break Management**: Automatic short breaks (5 min) and long breaks (15 min)
- **Session Tracking**: All sessions are saved to your history
- **Digital Blackboard**: Draw, write, and take notes during your session
- **Session History**: View all past sessions with details
- **Minimize/Expand**: Minimize sessions to continue working while the timer runs
- **Customizable Settings**: Adjust study time, break durations, and visual themes

### How to Start a Session

1. Click **"Study Session"** in the sidebar or Home page.
2. **Enter Session Details**:
   - **Subject/Topic**: What you're studying (e.g., "Math Chapter 5")
   - **Session Notes**: Optional notes about your study goals
3. **Choose a Study Technique**:
   - **Pomodoro** (25 min): Classic technique with built-in breaks
   - **Short Session** (15 min): Quick focused bursts
   - **Medium Session** (45 min): Balanced study time
   - **Long Session** (90 min): Extended deep focus
   - **Custom**: Set your own duration (1-180 minutes)
4. Click **"Start Session"**.

### During a Session

**Timer Controls:**
- **⏸️/▶️**: Pause or resume the timer
- **⏭️**: Skip break (only available during breaks)
- **⏹️**: Stop entire session (ends both study and break)
- **⚙️**: Open session settings
- **←**: Minimize session (timer continues running)

**Break Management:**
- When your study timer ends, you'll automatically be prompted to take a break
- Choose **Short Break** (5 min) or **Long Break** (15 min)
- During breaks, you can:
  - Skip the break to continue studying
  - Let it run and return to study automatically
  - Stop the entire session

**Using the Blackboard:**
1. Click the **"Board"** button on the right edge of the session screen
2. **Tools Available**:
   - **✏️ Pen**: Draw with your mouse or touch
   - **🧹 Eraser**: Erase parts of your drawing
   - **A Text**: Add text (click, then enter text when prompted)
   - **Color Picker**: Choose white, blue, green, orange, or red
   - **↶ Undo**: Undo last action
   - **🗑️ Clear**: Clear the entire board (requires confirmation)
3. **Mini Timer**: The timer continues running in the top-right corner
4. Click **"← Back to Session"** to return to the main timer view

### Ending a Session

**Two Ways to End:**

1. **Let it Complete**: When the timer reaches 0:00, the session automatically completes
2. **Stop Manually**: Click **⏹️ Stop Entire Session** (requires confirmation)

**Important Differences:**
- **Stopping** saves the session with "stopped" status and records actual time studied
- **Completing** saves the session with "completed" status
- Both are saved to your history and count toward your statistics

### Session History

After ending a session, you'll see:

- **Total Sessions**: Count of all your study sessions
- **Total Minutes**: Sum of all study time
- **Average Duration**: Average length of your sessions
- **Session List**: All past sessions showing:
  - Subject/topic
  - Date and time
  - Duration
  - Status (completed or stopped)
  - Notes (if added)

**Managing History:**
- Click **🗑️** on any session to delete it (requires confirmation)
- Deleted sessions are removed from your statistics

### Minimizing Sessions

**To Minimize:**
1. Click the **←** button in the top-left during an active session
2. The session becomes a floating timer that you can drag around your screen
3. The timer continues counting down

**While Minimized:**
- Timer keeps running in the background
- You can navigate to other pages
- Click the floating timer to expand it back
- Click **✕** on the floating timer to end the session (same as Stop Entire Session)

**Important:** Minimizing doesn't pause the timer. It continues running, so your session will complete even if you're on another page.

### Session Settings

Click **⚙️** during a session to customize:

- **Study Time**: Default Pomodoro duration (default: 25 minutes)
- **Short Break**: Duration for short breaks (default: 5 minutes)
- **Long Break**: Duration for long breaks (default: 15 minutes)
- **Gradient Theme**: Visual theme for your session screen

Settings apply to future sessions and your current session's theme.

### Edge Cases & Important Behaviors

- **Leaving vs Ending**: 
  - **Minimizing** (← button) = Timer continues, session stays active
  - **Stopping** (⏹️ button) = Session ends, saved to history
- **Break Behavior**: 
  - Study time is paused during breaks
  - Skipping a break returns you to study with remaining time
  - Stopping during a break ends the entire session
- **Session Restoration**: 
  - If you close the app during a session, it may restore when you return (if less than 24 hours old)
  - Expired or invalid sessions are automatically cleared
- **Notifications**: 
  - Enable browser notifications to get alerts when sessions complete
  - Click "Enable Notifications" button if prompted

## Study Together (Collaborative)

### Purpose

Schedule and participate in synchronized study sessions with friends. Everyone studies together with shared timers, chat, and a collaborative blackboard.

### Features

- **Schedule Sessions**: Plan study sessions with friends in advance
- **Waiting Room**: See who's ready before starting
- **Synchronized Timer**: Everyone sees the same countdown
- **Session Chat**: Real-time messaging during study
- **Collaborative Blackboard**: Draw and share ideas together
- **Participant Management**: See who's studying with you
- **Session History**: View past group sessions

### How to Schedule a Session

1. Go to **"Study Together"** from the sidebar or Home page.
2. Click **"Schedule Session"** button.
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Select Friend**: Choose from your friends list (must have friends first)
   - **Subject**: What you'll be studying together
   - **Study Type**: Pomodoro, Deep Focus, Study Group, or Custom
   - **Duration**: Length in minutes (15-480)
   - **Date**: When to study
   - **Time**: What time to start
4. Click **"Schedule"**.

**Note:** Your friend will receive a pending request and must accept it before the session is confirmed.

### Pending Requests

**Viewing Requests:**
1. Click the **"Pending Requests"** tab
2. You'll see all study invitations sent to you

**Responding to Requests:**
- **Accept**: Click **"Accept"** to join the session
- **Decline**: Click **"Decline"** to reject (the other person is notified)

**Important:** Only accepted sessions appear in "Upcoming Sessions."

### Upcoming Sessions

**Viewing Upcoming Sessions:**
1. Click the **"Upcoming Sessions"** tab
2. See all accepted sessions you're part of

**Session Status:**
- **⏳ Pending**: Waiting for acceptance
- **✅ Accepted**: Confirmed, ready to join when scheduled time arrives

**Actions Available:**
- **Join**: Enter the waiting room (available when session time arrives or if already active)
- **Rejoin**: Return to an active session you left
- **Cancel**: Remove the session (only if you're the creator)

### The Waiting Room

**Purpose:** A pre-session area where participants gather and indicate they're ready.

**How It Works:**
1. When you **Join** a scheduled session, you enter the waiting room
2. **See Participants**: View all friends who have joined
3. **Ready Status**: 
   - Toggle the "I'm Ready" checkbox to show you're prepared
   - See who else is ready (green checkmark)
4. **Countdown**: See time remaining until the session can start

**Starting the Session:**
- **Only the creator** can start the session
- The "Start Session" button appears when:
  - The scheduled time has arrived
  - All participants are ready (optional, depends on session settings)
- Once started, everyone enters the synchronized study session

**Leaving the Waiting Room:**
- Click **"Leave Session"** to exit (others can continue)
- You can rejoin later if the session hasn't started

### Active Study Session

**Features Available:**

**Timer Display:**
- Large countdown timer (synchronized for all participants)
- Shows study type and subject
- Pause indicator if someone paused

**Controls:**
- **⏸️/▶️ Pause/Resume**: Pause affects everyone (anyone can pause/resume)
- **⏹️ Stop Session**: Ends for everyone (only creator can stop)
- **🚪 Leave Session**: You leave, others continue
- **⚙️ Settings**: Personal settings (theme, etc.)
- **← Minimize**: Minimize to floating timer

**Participants Panel:**
- Click the participant count to see who's studying
- Shows:
  - Names and avatars
  - Who's the creator (👑)
  - Who's you (You badge)
  - Status indicators

**Session Chat:**
1. Click the **💬** button (bottom-right)
2. **Unread Badge**: Red number shows unread messages when chat is closed
3. **Send Messages**: Type and press Enter or click Send
4. **View Messages**: See all messages with sender names and timestamps
5. **Close Chat**: Click X to minimize (unread count updates when new messages arrive)

**Important Chat Behaviors:**
- Messages are real-time (updates every 2 seconds)
- Unread count resets when you open chat
- Chat persists even if you minimize the session

**Collaborative Blackboard:**
1. Click the **"Board"** button on the right edge
2. **Shared Canvas**: Everyone sees the same drawing in real-time
3. **Tools**: Same as solo sessions (pen, eraser, text, colors)
4. **Synchronization**: 
   - Your drawings appear for others within 1 second
   - Others' drawings appear for you automatically
   - Changes are saved continuously
5. **Clear Warning**: Clearing affects everyone (requires confirmation)

**Mini Timer on Blackboard:**
- Timer continues in the top-right corner
- Shows current time and pause status
- Click "← Back to Session" to return

### Leaving vs Ending Sessions

**Leaving a Session:**
- Click **🚪 Leave Session**
- **You leave**, but others continue studying
- You can rejoin later if the session is still active
- Your study time is not recorded (you left early)

**Ending a Session:**
- Click **⏹️ Stop Session** (creator only)
- **Everyone's session ends**
- All participants are notified
- Study time is recorded for everyone who participated

**Important:** If you're not the creator, you can only leave, not end the session for everyone.

### Rejoining Sessions

**How to Rejoin:**
1. Go to **"Study Together"** → **"Upcoming Sessions"**
2. Find the active session (status shows "active")
3. Click **"Rejoin"**
4. You'll return to the synchronized session with the current timer

**What's Preserved:**
- Timer continues from where it was
- Chat messages are still visible
- Blackboard drawings remain
- Your participant status is restored

### Minimizing Group Sessions

**To Minimize:**
1. Click **←** during an active session
2. Session becomes a floating timer
3. Timer continues running (synchronized with others)

**While Minimized:**
- You can navigate to other pages
- Timer keeps counting down
- Others can still see you as a participant
- Click floating timer to expand
- Click **✕** to leave the session (same as Leave Session button)

**Important:** Minimizing doesn't pause the timer for others. The session continues normally.

### Edge Cases & Important Behaviors

- **Session Creator Privileges**: 
  - Only the creator can start the session from waiting room
  - Only the creator can stop the session for everyone
  - Anyone can pause/resume the timer
- **Timer Synchronization**: 
  - Timers sync every 10 seconds
  - Small differences (< 60 seconds) are normal
  - Large differences trigger automatic sync
- **Network Issues**: 
  - If you disconnect, you can rejoin when connection is restored
  - Timer continues on server, so you'll sync when you return
- **Multiple Devices**: 
  - You can join from multiple devices
  - All devices see the same timer and chat
- **Session Completion**: 
  - When timer reaches 0:00, session completes for everyone
  - All participants see completion message
  - Session is saved to history for all participants

## Analytics

### Purpose

View detailed statistics about your study habits, track progress over time, and identify patterns in your learning.

### Features

- **Date Range Selection**: View last 7 days, 30 days, or custom range
- **Summary Statistics**: Total study time, sessions, averages, and longest session
- **Study Streaks**: Current and longest consecutive study days
- **Daily Study Chart**: Visual graph of study time per day
- **Subject Breakdown**: Pie chart and list showing time spent per subject
- **Configurable Streak Minimum**: Set minimum study time to count as a "study day"

### How to Use

**Viewing Analytics:**
1. Click **"Analytics"** in the sidebar or Home page
2. **Select Date Range**:
   - **Last 7 Days**: Past week
   - **Last 30 Days**: Past month
   - **Custom Range**: Pick specific start and end dates

**Understanding the Statistics:**

**Summary Cards:**
- **⏱️ Total Study Time**: Hours and minutes studied in the selected period
- **📚 Total Sessions**: Number of study sessions completed
- **📊 Average Session**: Average duration of your sessions
- **🏆 Longest Session**: Your longest single study session

**Study Streaks:**
- **Current Streak**: Consecutive days you've studied (ending today or yesterday)
- **Longest Streak**: Your best consecutive study period
- **Streak Minimum**: Set how many minutes count as a "study day" (default: 20 minutes)
  - Adjust the slider to change what counts (1-120 minutes)
  - Lower = easier to maintain streaks
  - Higher = more rigorous streak requirements

**Daily Study Chart:**
- Bar chart showing study time for each day
- Hover over bars to see exact minutes
- Helps identify your most productive days

**Subject Breakdown:**
- **Pie Chart**: Visual representation of time per subject
- **Subject List**: Detailed breakdown with:
  - Subject name
  - Time in hours and minutes
  - Visual bar showing relative time
- Subjects are automatically categorized from your session notes

### Important Notes

- **Data Source**: Analytics use data from your Study Session (solo) history
- **Study Together Sessions**: Currently not included in analytics (may be added in future)
- **Subject Detection**: Subjects are extracted from your session "Subject/Topic" field
- **Uncategorized**: Sessions without subjects appear as "Uncategorized"
- **Streak Calculation**: 
  - Only counts days with at least the minimum study time
  - Must be consecutive days (no gaps)
  - Today counts if you've studied today

### Edge Cases

- **No Data**: If you haven't studied in the selected range, you'll see empty states
- **Custom Date Range**: Both start and end dates must be selected
- **Timezone**: All dates use your local timezone
- **Deleted Sessions**: Deleted sessions are removed from analytics immediately

## Schedule Planner

### Purpose

Upload your class timetable and generate an optimized weekly study schedule that fits around your classes, extracurriculars, and personal preferences.

### Features

- **Timetable Upload**: Upload images or paste text of your class schedule
- **AI Extraction**: Automatically extract classes from timetables
- **Manual Class Entry**: Add or edit classes manually
- **Constraint Settings**: Set wake time, sleep time, commute, study hours, etc.
- **Extracurricular Activities**: Add sports, clubs, or other activities
- **AI Schedule Generation**: Generate optimized study schedule
- **Drag & Drop Editing**: Rearrange activities by dragging
- **PDF Export**: Download your schedule as a PDF
- **Intensity Levels**: Light, Balanced, or Intense study schedules

### How to Use

**Step 1: Enter Your Timetable**

**Option A - Upload Image:**
1. Click **"Upload Timetable"**
2. Select an image file (PNG, JPG) of your timetable
3. The AI will extract class information automatically
4. Text files (.txt) are also supported

**Option B - Paste Text:**
1. Paste your timetable text in the text area
2. Format: "Monday 9:00-10:00 CS4297 Room 101"
3. Include day, time, subject, and location if possible

**Option C - Manual Entry:**
1. Click **"Enter Manually"**
2. You'll go directly to the class confirmation step

**Extract Classes:**
- Click **"Extract Classes"** after uploading or pasting
- Wait for AI processing (may take 10-30 seconds)
- Review the extracted classes in the next step

**Step 2: Confirm Your Classes**

**Review Extracted Classes:**
- Each class shows: Day, Time, Subject, Location, Type, Weeks
- **Edit**: Click "Edit" to modify any class
- **Delete**: Click "Delete" to remove incorrect classes
- **Add Class**: Click "+ Add Class" to add missing classes

**Editing Classes:**
- Change any field (day, time, subject, etc.)
- Click "Save" when done
- Click "Delete" to remove the class

**Continue:**
- Click **"Continue to Constraints"** when all classes are correct
- You must have at least one class to continue

**Step 3: Set Your Constraints**

**Basic Settings:**
- **Commute Time**: Minutes to get to campus (default: 30)
- **Wake Time**: When you typically wake up (default: 07:00)
- **Sleep Time**: When you go to bed (default: 23:00)
- **Study Hours Per Day**: Target hours of study (default: 3)
- **Allow Late-Night Study**: Toggle if you study late (after sleep time)

**Extracurricular Activities:**
- Click **"+ Add Extracurricular Activity"**
- Enter: Day, Start Time, End Time, Activity Name, Location
- Add multiple activities (gym, clubs, etc.)
- Click "Remove" to delete activities

**Generate Schedule:**
- Click **"Generate Schedule"** when ready
- AI creates an optimized schedule based on your constraints
- Processing takes 10-30 seconds

**Step 4: Your Weekly Schedule**

**Viewing Your Schedule:**
- Seven columns (Monday-Sunday)
- Each day shows all activities:
  - **Classes** (blue): Your actual classes
  - **Study** (green): Recommended study blocks
  - **Extracurricular** (orange): Your activities
  - **Break** (gray): Rest periods
  - **Commute** (purple): Travel time

**Activity Details:**
- Time range (e.g., "09:00 - 10:00")
- Type badge
- Subject/title
- Location (if applicable)
- Notes (if added)

**Editing Your Schedule:**

**Drag & Drop:**
1. Click and hold the **⋮⋮** handle on any activity
2. Drag to a new position (same day or different day)
3. Drop in a valid time slot
4. Schedule updates automatically

**Edit Activity:**
1. Click **✏️** on any activity
2. Modify: Day, Time, Type, Subject, Location, Notes
3. Click "Save" (validates no time conflicts)
4. Click "Cancel" to discard changes

**Delete Activity:**
1. Click **🗑️** on any activity
2. Confirm deletion
3. Activity is removed

**Add New Activity:**
1. Click **"+ Add New Activity"**
2. Fill in all fields:
   - Day, Start Time, End Time (required)
   - Type, Subject, Location, Notes (optional)
3. Click "Add Activity"
4. Activity is added and validated for conflicts

**Regenerate Schedule:**
- Click intensity buttons: **Light**, **Balanced**, or **Intense**
- **Light**: Fewer study blocks, more breaks
- **Balanced**: Moderate study time (default)
- **Intense**: Maximum study blocks, minimal breaks
- Schedule regenerates with new intensity

**Export as PDF:**
1. Click **"📥 Download as PDF"**
2. PDF includes:
   - Header with generation date
   - Full weekly schedule
   - All activities with details
   - Footer with StudyFocus branding
3. File downloads as "StudyFocus-Schedule-[date].pdf"

### Important Behaviors

**Time Validation:**
- Activities cannot overlap
- Start time must be before end time
- Activities must fit within wake/sleep times (unless late-night is allowed)
- Invalid placements show error messages

**Drag & Drop Rules:**
- Can move activities between days
- Must drop in valid time slots (no overlaps)
- Invalid drops are rejected with explanation
- Activities auto-sort by time after placement

**Schedule Generation:**
- AI considers all constraints
- Adds buffer time between activities
- Places study sessions in optimal gaps
- Respects your preferences (wake, sleep, commute)

**Data Persistence:**
- Schedule is auto-saved as you edit
- Returns to last step when you revisit
- Classes and constraints are preserved

### Edge Cases

- **Empty Timetable**: Can still create schedule with just constraints
- **Time Conflicts**: System prevents overlapping activities
- **Invalid Times**: Must have valid start/end times
- **PDF Export**: Large schedules may take longer to generate
- **AI Extraction Errors**: Can manually correct or re-extract

## Friends

### Purpose

Build your study network by connecting with friends. Friends can study together, chat, and see each other on the leaderboard.

### Features

- **Send Friend Requests**: Invite friends by email
- **Accept/Decline Requests**: Manage incoming invitations
- **Friend List**: View all your connections
- **Quick Chat**: Start messaging directly from friends list
- **Remove Friends**: Unfriend if needed

### How to Add Friends

**Sending a Request:**
1. Go to **"Friends"** from the sidebar or Home page
2. Click **"+ Add Friend"** button
3. Enter your friend's **email address**
4. Click **"Send Request"**
5. Your friend receives a pending request notification

**Important:** Your friend must have a StudyFocus account with that email address.

### Managing Friend Requests

**Pending Requests (Received):**
- View all requests others have sent you
- Shows: Sender name, email, and date sent
- **Actions:**
  - **✓ Accept**: Add them as a friend
  - **✗ Decline**: Reject the request

**After Accepting:**
- Friend appears in your "My Friends" list
- You can now:
  - Study together
  - Send messages
  - See them on leaderboard

### Your Friends List

**Viewing Friends:**
- See all accepted friends
- Shows: Name, email, and date added
- Empty state if you have no friends yet

**Friend Actions:**
- **💬 Chat**: Click to open Messages with that friend
- **🗑️ Remove**: Unfriend (requires confirmation)

**Important:** Removing a friend:
- Removes them from your list
- They're removed from your friends list too
- You can send a new request to reconnect later

### Important Behaviors

- **Email Must Match**: Friend's email must exactly match their account
- **One Request at a Time**: Can't send duplicate requests
- **Mutual Friendship**: Accepting creates a mutual connection
- **No Self-Requests**: Can't send requests to yourself

### Edge Cases

- **Friend Not Found**: Error if email doesn't have an account
- **Already Friends**: Error if already connected
- **Pending Request Exists**: Error if request already sent
- **Network Errors**: Check connection and try again

## Messages

### Purpose

Chat with your friends in real-time. Send messages, share files, and stay connected with your study partners.

### Features

- **Conversation List**: See all your chats
- **Real-Time Messaging**: Messages update automatically
- **File Sharing**: Send images, PDFs, and other files (up to 10MB)
- **Unread Counts**: See how many unread messages you have
- **Message History**: All conversations are saved
- **File Downloads**: Download shared files

### How to Use

**Starting a Conversation:**
1. Go to **"Messages"** from the sidebar
2. **Option A**: Click **💬** next to a friend in the Friends page
3. **Option B**: Click an existing conversation in Messages
4. If no conversation exists, it's created when you send the first message

**Sending Messages:**
1. Select a conversation (or start new from Friends)
2. Type your message in the input box
3. Press **Enter** or click **➤** to send
4. **Shift+Enter** for new line

**Sending Files:**
1. Click the **📎** attachment button
2. Select a file from your device (max 10MB)
3. File appears in preview above input
4. Type optional message text
5. Press **Enter** or click **➤** to send
6. **Remove File**: Click **×** on preview to cancel

**File Types Supported:**
- Images (PNG, JPG, GIF, etc.) - Opens in new tab
- PDFs - Downloads
- Documents (Word, Excel, etc.) - Downloads
- Other files - Downloads

**Viewing Messages:**
- **Your Messages**: Appear on the right (blue/green)
- **Their Messages**: Appear on the left (gray)
- **Timestamps**: Show time for recent messages, date for older
- **File Messages**: Show file icon, name, and size
- **Auto-Scroll**: New messages automatically scroll into view

**Downloading Files:**
1. Click the file name in a message
2. **Images**: Open in new browser tab
3. **Other Files**: Download to your device

### Unread Messages

**Unread Count Badge:**
- Red number badge on **💬 Messages** in sidebar
- Shows total unread messages across all conversations
- Updates in real-time
- Resets to 0 when you open Messages page

**Marking as Read:**
- Messages are automatically marked read when you:
  - Open the conversation
  - View the Messages page
- Unread count updates immediately

**Unread Indicators:**
- Conversations with unread messages are highlighted
- Unread count badge on each conversation
- Badge shows number of unread messages in that chat

### Conversation List

**Viewing All Conversations:**
- List shows all friends you've messaged
- Sorted by most recent message
- Shows:
  - Friend's name
  - Last message preview
  - Timestamp of last message
  - Unread count badge (if any)

**Empty State:**
- Shows if you have no conversations yet
- Start by clicking 💬 on a friend in Friends page

**Navigating Conversations:**
- Click any conversation to open it
- Click **←** to return to conversation list
- Selected conversation is highlighted

### Important Behaviors

- **Real-Time Updates**: Messages poll every 2 seconds for new messages
- **Message Persistence**: All messages are saved permanently
- **File Size Limit**: 10MB maximum per file
- **Auto-Focus**: Input automatically focuses when conversation opens
- **Message Ordering**: Messages sorted by timestamp (oldest to newest)

### Edge Cases

- **Network Issues**: Messages queue and send when connection restored
- **Large Files**: Files over 10MB are rejected with error message
- **File Upload Errors**: Error message shown, file not sent
- **Empty Conversations**: Can send first message to start conversation
- **Friend Removed**: Conversations remain, but friend may not respond

## Leaderboard

### Purpose

See how your study performance compares with other StudyFocus users. Track your ranking and motivate yourself to study more.

### Features

- **Study Score**: Calculated from sessions, time, and completion rate
- **Ranking**: Your position among all users

## Floating Timer

### Purpose

Continue tracking your study session while working on other tasks. The floating timer lets you minimize sessions and keep them running in the background.

### Features

- **Draggable**: Move timer anywhere on screen
- **Always Visible**: Stays on top of other windows
- **Live Updates**: Shows current time remaining
- **Quick Actions**: Expand or close with one click
- **State Display**: Shows if studying, on break, or paused

### How It Works

**When It Appears:**
- Automatically appears when you minimize a study session
- Works for both solo and group study sessions
- Only one floating timer at a time

**Timer Display:**
- **Large Time**: Current time remaining (MM:SS format)
- **State**: "Study", "Break", or "Paused"
- **Subject**: What you're studying (if set)

**Moving the Timer:**
1. Click and drag the timer window
2. Release to place it
3. Position is remembered

**Actions Available:**
- **⛶ Expand**: Click to return to full session view
- **✕ Close**: Click to end the session (same as Stop/Leave)

### Important Behaviors

**Solo Sessions:**
- **Expand**: Returns to Study Session page, restores full view
- **Close (✕)**: Ends the session (same as "Stop Entire Session" button)
- **Confirmation**: Closing asks for confirmation before ending

**Group Sessions:**
- **Expand**: Returns to Study Together active session
- **Close (✕)**: Leaves the session (same as "Leave Session" button)
- **Confirmation**: Closing asks for confirmation before leaving

**Timer Continuity:**
- Timer **continues running** when minimized
- Counts down in real-time
- Updates every second
- Syncs with server (for group sessions)

**Navigation:**
- You can navigate to any page while timer is floating
- Timer stays visible and functional
- Click expand to return to session

### Edge Cases

- **Multiple Sessions**: Only one floating timer (most recent)
- **Page Refresh**: Floating timer state may be lost (session continues on server)
- **Browser Close**: Session may restore when you return (if recent)
- **Timer Completion**: Timer disappears when session completes

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Session Not Starting:**
- Check that you've entered a subject
- Ensure you've selected a study technique
- Try refreshing the page
- Check browser console for errors

**Timer Not Counting:**
- Ensure session is not paused
- Check that you haven't minimized and forgotten
- Refresh page to restore session state
- Verify browser allows JavaScript

**Messages Not Sending:**
- Check internet connection
- Verify file size is under 10MB
- Try refreshing the page
- Check that friend is still in your friends list

**Schedule Not Generating:**
- Ensure you have at least one class entered
- Check that constraints are set
- Wait for AI processing (may take 30+ seconds)
- Try regenerating with different intensity

**Can't Add Friend:**
- Verify email address is correct
- Ensure friend has a StudyFocus account
- Check that you're not already friends
- Verify no pending request exists

**Analytics Showing No Data:**
- Check date range selection
- Ensure you have completed study sessions
- Verify sessions are in the selected date range
- Check that sessions weren't deleted

**Floating Timer Disappeared:**
- Session may have completed
- Check if you accidentally closed it
- Navigate to Study Session or Study Together to see active session
- Session may have expired (over 24 hours old)

### Getting Help

If you encounter issues not covered here:

1. **Check Settings → About → Help & Support**
2. **Report Bug**: Use the bug report feature
3. **Contact Support**: Reach out through support channels
4. **Review This Guide**: Many answers are in the detailed sections above
