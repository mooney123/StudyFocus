## Getting Started

### 1. How do I create a StudyFocus account?

To create an account, click "Sign Up" on the login page. Enter your full name, email address, and a password (minimum 6 characters). After clicking "Create Account," you'll be automatically logged in and taken to the Home page. Your email address will be used for login, so make sure it's valid.

### 2. Can I use StudyFocus without creating an account?

No, you need to create an account to use StudyFocus. This ensures your study data is saved and you can access features like Friends, Study Together, and personalized Analytics. Your account is free and only requires an email address.

## Study Sessions

### 3. What study techniques are available in Study Session?

StudyFocus offers five study techniques:
- **Pomodoro** (25 minutes): Classic technique with built-in breaks
- **Short Session** (15 minutes): Quick focused bursts
- **Medium Session** (45 minutes): Balanced study time
- **Long Session** (90 minutes): Extended deep focus
- **Custom**: Set your own duration between 1-180 minutes

You can also customize break durations and study time in session settings.

### 4. What is the digital blackboard and how do I use it?

The digital blackboard is a drawing and note-taking tool available during study sessions. Click the "Board" button on the right edge of the session screen to access it. You can:
- Draw with a pen tool
- Erase parts of your drawing
- Add text annotations
- Choose from multiple colors (white, blue, green, orange, red)
- Undo actions or clear the entire board

The timer continues running in the top-right corner while you use the blackboard.

### 5. What happens if I minimize a study session?

When you minimize a session (click the ← button), it becomes a floating timer that you can drag around your screen. The timer continues running in the background, so your session will complete even if you navigate to other pages. You can click the floating timer to expand it back, or click ✕ to end the session. Minimizing does not pause the timer.

## Study Together

### 6. How do I schedule a study session with friends?

Go to "Study Together" and click "Schedule Session." Fill in:
- Select a friend from your friends list
- Subject you'll be studying
- Study type (Pomodoro, Deep Focus, Study Group, or Custom)
- Duration (15-480 minutes)
- Date and time

Your friend will receive a pending request and must accept it before the session is confirmed.

### 7. What's the difference between leaving and ending a Study Together session?

**Leaving a session** (🚪 Leave Session): You leave, but others continue studying. You can rejoin later if the session is still active. Your study time is not recorded.

**Ending a session** (⏹️ Stop Session): Only the session creator can do this. It ends the session for everyone, and all participants' study time is recorded. All participants are notified when the session ends.

### 8. How does the collaborative blackboard work?

The collaborative blackboard is shared in real-time with all participants. When you draw or write, others see your changes within 1 second, and you see theirs automatically. Everyone has access to the same tools (pen, eraser, text, colors). If someone clears the board, it affects everyone and requires confirmation.

## Analytics & Tracking

### 9. How are study streaks calculated?

Study streaks count consecutive days you've studied. You can set a minimum study time (default: 20 minutes) that counts as a "study day." The streak:
- Only counts days with at least the minimum study time
- Must be consecutive (no gaps)
- Today counts if you've studied today
- Breaks if you miss a day

Adjust the streak minimum slider (1-120 minutes) in Analytics to change what counts.

### 10. Why don't my Study Together sessions appear in Analytics?

Currently, Analytics only includes data from solo Study Session history. Study Together sessions are tracked separately and may be added to Analytics in a future update. For now, only individual study sessions count toward your analytics, streaks, and subject breakdown.

### 11. How does StudyFocus categorize subjects in Analytics?

Subjects are automatically extracted from the "Subject/Topic" field you enter when starting a study session. The system groups sessions by subject name, so be consistent with naming (e.g., "Math" vs "Mathematics" will be separate categories). Sessions without subjects appear as "Uncategorized."

## Schedule Planner

### 12. Can I use Schedule Planner without uploading a timetable?

Yes! You can click "Enter Manually" to skip timetable upload and go directly to adding classes manually. You can also create a schedule with just constraints (wake time, sleep time, study hours) and extracurricular activities, then let the AI generate study blocks around your preferences.

### 13. What are the different intensity levels for schedule generation?

Schedule Planner offers three intensity levels:
- **Light**: Fewer study blocks, more breaks between activities
- **Balanced**: Moderate study time (default setting)
- **Intense**: Maximum study blocks, minimal breaks

You can regenerate your schedule with different intensity levels after initial generation. The AI will adjust the number and placement of study sessions accordingly.

## Friends & Messaging

### 14. How do I add friends on StudyFocus?

Go to "Friends" and click "+ Add Friend." Enter your friend's email address (must match their StudyFocus account exactly) and click "Send Request." Your friend will receive a pending request notification and must accept it before you become friends. You can't send requests to yourself or to someone you're already friends with.

### 15. What file types can I send in Messages?

You can send any file type up to 10MB in size. Images (PNG, JPG, GIF, etc.) will open in a new browser tab when clicked. PDFs, documents (Word, Excel, etc.), and other files will download to the recipient's device. If a file exceeds 10MB, you'll receive an error message and the file won't be sent.

## Leaderboard

### 16. How is my study score calculated?

Your study score (out of 100) is calculated based on:
- **Total Sessions**: More sessions = higher score
- **Total Study Time**: More minutes studied = higher score
- **Average Duration**: Longer average sessions = higher score
- **Completion Rate**: Higher percentage of completed sessions = higher score

The score updates in real-time as you complete more study sessions. Study levels range from Getting Started (0-19) to Master (80-100).

### 17. Can I hide my stats from the leaderboard?

Yes! Go to Settings → Privacy and toggle "Study Stats Visibility" off. When disabled, your statistics will be hidden from the leaderboard and friends. Your data is still tracked privately, but others won't see your performance.

## StudyFocus AI

### 18. What data does StudyFocus AI have access to?

StudyFocus AI only has access to your study session history (last 7 days by default). It cannot see:
- Other users' data
- Your messages or conversations
- Your friends list
- Your schedule details
- Any personal information beyond study session data

The AI uses this data to provide personalized insights and recommendations about your study habits. All your data remains private and secure.

## Settings & Customization

### 19. Can I change the language in StudyFocus?

Yes! Go to Settings → Language and select from:
- **English** (default)
- **中文 (Chinese)** - Simplified Chinese interface

The interface updates immediately when you change languages, and your preference is saved. Note that some proper nouns and technical terms may not translate.

## Technical & Troubleshooting

### 20. My timer stopped counting. What should I do?

First, check that the session isn't paused (look for the pause icon). If you minimized the session, check if the floating timer is still visible. Try refreshing the page to restore session state. If the problem persists, check your browser's JavaScript settings and ensure you have a stable internet connection.
