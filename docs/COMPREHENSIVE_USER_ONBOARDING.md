## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Creating Your Account

**Step-by-Step:**

1. **Open StudyFocus** in your web browser
2. You'll see the **Sign Up** page automatically
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Full Name**: Enter your name as you'd like it to appear to friends
   - **Email Address**: This will be your login username
   - **Password**: Must be at least 6 characters long
   - **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to verify
4. Click **"Create Account"**
5. You'll be automatically logged in and taken to the Home page

**Important Notes:**
- Your password must be at least 6 characters
- Passwords must match in both fields
- You'll stay logged in between sessions (no need to log in every time)

### 1.2 Logging In

If you already have an account:

1. Click **"Sign In"** at the bottom of the signup form
2. Enter your **Email Address** and **Password**
3. Click **"Sign In"**
4. You'll be taken to your Home page

**Tip:** Your session persists, so you won't need to log in every time you visit.

### 1.3 First-Time Experience

When you first log in, you'll see:

1. **Welcome Modal**: A welcome screen introducing StudyFocus
   - Click **"Get Started"** to begin
   - This only appears once

2. **Tab Hints**: As you explore different pages, helpful hints will appear
   - These explain what each page does
   - Click **"Got it"** to dismiss
   - Hints won't appear again once dismissed

3. **Feature Tooltips**: When you first use certain features, tooltips will guide you
   - These provide contextual help
   - Dismiss them when you're ready

## 3. Study Session (Solo)

### 3.1 Overview

Study Session lets you track individual study time with Pomodoro timers, breaks, and a digital blackboard.

### 3.2 Starting a Session

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Navigate to **Study Session** (sidebar or Home page)
2. **Enter Session Details:**
   - **Subject/Topic**: What you're studying (e.g., "Math Chapter 5")
   - **Session Notes**: Optional notes about your goals
3. **Choose Study Technique:**
   - **Pomodoro** (25 min): Classic technique with automatic breaks
   - **Short** (15 min): Quick focused session
   - **Medium** (45 min): Standard session
   - **Long** (90 min): Extended session
   - **Custom**: Set your own duration
4. Click **"Start Session"**
5. Timer begins counting down

### 3.3 During a Session

**Timer Controls:**
- **⏸️ Pause**: Pause the timer (time is preserved)
- **▶️ Resume**: Continue from where you paused
- **⏹️ Stop**: End the session early (confirmation required)
- **➖ Minimize**: Minimize to floating timer (continue working while timer runs)

**Break Management:**
- After a work session, a **break timer** starts automatically
- **Short breaks**: 5 minutes (after each Pomodoro)
- **Long breaks**: 15 minutes (after 4 Pomodoros)
- You can skip breaks if needed

**Blackboard:**
- Click **"Open Blackboard"** to access drawing canvas
- **Tools:**
  - **Pen**: Draw freely
  - **Eraser**: Erase parts of your drawing
  - **Text**: Add text notes
- **Color Picker**: Choose drawing color
- **Undo/Redo**: Navigate drawing history
- **Save**: Your blackboard state is saved automatically

### 3.4 Session History

**Viewing History:**
- Scroll down to see **Session History**
- Each session shows:
  - Subject/topic
  - Date and time
  - Duration
  - Status (completed/stopped)
  - Notes (if added)

**Session Details:**
- Click on a session to view full details
- See your notes and blackboard from that session

### 3.5 Session Settings

**Customizing Timers:**
1. Click **⚙️ Settings** button
2. Adjust:
   - **Work Time**: Default study duration (minutes)
   - **Break Time**: Short break duration (minutes)
   - **Long Break Time**: Extended break duration (minutes)
   - **Notifications**: Enable/disable timer notifications
3. Settings are saved automatically

### 3.6 Floating Timer

When you minimize a session:
- A **floating timer** appears in the corner
- Shows remaining time
- Works across all tabs
- Click to **expand** back to full view
- Click **✕** to end session (confirmation required)

**Tip:** Use floating timer to continue browsing while keeping track of study time.

## 4. Study Together (Collaborative)

### 4.1 Overview

Study Together lets you schedule and join synchronized study sessions with friends.

### 4.2 Scheduling a Session

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Navigate to **Study Together**
2. Click **"Schedule Session"** button
3. Fill in details:
   - **Friend**: Select from your friends list
   - **Subject**: What you'll study together
   - **Study Type**: Pomodoro, Short, Medium, Long, or Custom
   - **Duration**: Length of session (minutes)
   - **Date**: When to study
   - **Time**: What time to start
4. Click **"Schedule"**
5. Friend receives a **pending request**

### 4.3 Managing Requests

**Pending Requests (You Were Invited):**
- Appear in **"Pending Requests"** section
- Shows who invited you, subject, date, and time
- **Actions:**
  - **Accept**: Accept the invitation
  - **Decline**: Decline the invitation

**Your Scheduled Sessions:**
- Sessions you created or accepted appear in **"Scheduled Sessions"**
- Shows upcoming sessions
- **Actions:**
  - **Join**: Join when it's time
  - **Cancel**: Cancel if you're the creator

### 4.4 Joining a Session

**Step-by-Step:**

1. When it's time, click **"Join"** on a scheduled session
2. You'll enter the **Waiting Room**
3. **Toggle Ready Status:**
   - Click **"Ready"** when you're prepared
   - All participants must be ready to start
4. Once all are ready, **"Start Session"** button appears
5. Click to begin synchronized timer

### 4.5 During a Session

**Synchronized Timer:**
- Timer is synchronized across all participants
- Everyone sees the same time remaining
- **Pause/Resume**: When one person pauses, all see pause
- Timer updates in real-time

**Chat:**
- Click **"Chat"** to open chat panel
- Send messages to all participants
- Messages are saved and visible to all

**Shared Blackboard:**
- Click **"Blackboard"** to open shared canvas
- All participants can draw simultaneously
- Changes sync in real-time
- Use same tools as solo blackboard

**Leave/Stop:**
- **Leave**: Leave session but it continues for others
- **Stop**: End session for everyone (confirmation required)

### 4.6 Session States

- **Pending**: Waiting for friend to accept
- **Accepted**: Friend accepted, scheduled for future
- **Active**: Currently in progress
- **Completed**: Finished successfully
- **Cancelled**: Creator cancelled before start

## 5. Friends & Social Features

### 5.1 Adding Friends

**Step-by-Step:**

1. Navigate to **Friends**
2. Click **"Add Friend"** button
3. Enter friend's **email address**
4. Click **"Send Request"**
5. Friend receives notification

**Important:**
- Friend must have a StudyFocus account
- You can only send one request per person
- Friend must accept before you're connected

### 5.2 Managing Friend Requests

**Incoming Requests:**
- Appear in **"Pending Requests"** section
- Shows who sent the request
- **Actions:**
  - **Accept**: Add to friends list
  - **Decline**: Reject the request

**Outgoing Requests:**
- Shows requests you've sent
- Status: Pending until friend responds

### 5.3 Friends List

**Viewing Friends:**
- All accepted friends appear in **"Friends"** section
- Shows:
  - Friend's name
  - **Online Status**: 
    - 🟢 **Online**: Currently active
    - 🟡 **Away**: Inactive but logged in
    - ⚫ **Offline**: Not logged in
  - **Actions**: Remove friend

**Online Status:**
- Status updates in real-time
- Based on friend's activity
- Respects privacy settings (friends can hide status)

### 5.4 Removing Friends

1. Find friend in your **Friends** list
2. Click **"Remove"** button
3. Confirm removal
4. Friend is removed from both sides

**Note:** Removing a friend doesn't delete message history.

## 9. Schedule Planner

### 9.1 Overview

Schedule Planner helps you organize your weekly schedule, extract class timetables, and plan study time.

### 9.2 Input Methods

**Method 1: Paste Text**
1. Copy your timetable text
2. Paste into the text area
3. Click **"Extract Classes"**
4. Classes are automatically extracted

**Method 2: Upload Image**
1. Click **"Upload File"**
2. Select timetable image (PNG, JPG)
3. Image is processed using AI
4. Classes are automatically extracted

**Method 3: Upload PDF**
- PDFs are not directly supported
- Convert PDF to image first, or paste text

### 9.3 Editing Extracted Classes

**After Extraction:**
- Review extracted classes
- **Edit**: Click on a class to modify:
  - Day
  - Start/End time
  - Subject
  - Location
  - Type (lecture, lab, tutorial, etc.)
  - Weeks (which weeks it runs)
- **Add Class**: Click **"Add Class"** to manually add
- **Remove Class**: Click **✕** to delete

### 9.4 Setting Constraints

**Before Generating Schedule:**

1. Click **"Next"** after editing classes
2. Set **Constraints**:
   - **Commute Time**: Time between classes (minutes)
   - **Extracurriculars**: Add activities (e.g., "Gym 18:00-19:00")
   - **Wake Time**: When you wake up (e.g., 07:00)
   - **Sleep Time**: When you go to bed (e.g., 23:00)
   - **Study Hours Per Day**: Target study time (hours)
   - **Allow Late Study**: Enable study sessions after 22:00
3. Click **"Generate Schedule"**

### 9.5 Schedule Generation

**Intensity Levels:**
- **Light**: Fewer study blocks, more free time
- **Balanced**: Moderate study time distribution
- **Intense**: Maximum study time, fewer breaks

**Generated Schedule:**
- Shows weekly view (Monday-Sunday)
- Color-coded blocks:
  - **Classes**: Your extracted classes
  - **Study Time**: Suggested study blocks
  - **Extracurriculars**: Your activities
  - **Free Time**: Available time

### 9.6 Editing Your Schedule

**Drag and Drop:**
- Click and drag any block to move it
- Drop in new time slot
- Schedule updates automatically

**Edit Block:**
- Click on any block
- Modify time, subject, or type
- Changes save automatically

**Add Activity:**
- Click **"Add Activity"**
- Choose day and time
- Set type (study, break, activity)
- Add to schedule

### 9.7 Exporting Schedule

**Export to PDF:**
1. Click **"Export to PDF"** button
2. PDF downloads automatically
3. Includes full weekly schedule
4. Print or save for reference

## 14. Troubleshooting

### 14.1 Common Issues

**Timer Not Working:**
- Refresh the page
- Check if session is paused
- Try starting a new session
- Clear browser cache if persistent

**Messages Not Sending:**
- Check internet connection
- Verify friend is online
- Try refreshing the page
- Check notification settings

**Schedule Not Generating:**
- Ensure classes are properly extracted
- Check that constraints are set
- Verify date/time formats
- Try regenerating schedule

**Goals Not Updating:**
- Progress updates every 30 seconds
- Ensure you have active study sessions
- Check goal configuration
- Verify goal is active (not paused)

### 14.2 Data Issues

**Sessions Not Saving:**
- Data auto-saves every 2 seconds
- Check internet connection
- Verify you're logged in
- Try refreshing the page

**Friends Not Appearing:**
- Ensure friend accepted request
- Refresh friends list
- Check if friend account exists
- Verify email address is correct

**Leaderboard Empty:**
- Ensure you have friends
- Check privacy settings (yours and friends')
- Verify you have study sessions
